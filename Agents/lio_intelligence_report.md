Primary displacement risk comes from Zip expanding upmarket with agentic AI features in 2024-2025, potentially encroaching on Lio's target market.

**Win/Loss Risk:**

Enterprise procurement buyers prefer proven vendors — Lio must demonstrate 2-3 F500 case studies to compete in enterprise deals and establish credibility in the market. Additionally, Lio's ability to integrate with existing P2P and ERP systems will be a key factor in winning enterprise deals, as large organizations often have complex and entrenched procurement systems.
